## Planning is Fundamental
If you earn R$ 5,000 per month and spend R$ 1,500 on supermarket shopping, it's essential to know how to optimize these expenses. One of the main tips is to plan before going to the supermarket. This includes making a shopping list and avoiding buying things that are not necessary. Additionally, it's crucial to check the prices of products and choose the cheapest options.

For example, if you need to buy milk, bread, and eggs, check the prices of these products in different supermarkets or discount stores. You can save up to R$ 50 per month just by choosing the cheapest options.

## Choosing the Right Products
Another important tip is to choose products that are cheaper but still meet your needs. For example, instead of buying beef, you can opt for chicken or fish, which are cheaper. Additionally, it's essential to check the expiration date of products and choose the ones with a longer shelf life.

**Practical Tip:** Check the prices of products in different supermarkets and discount stores before making a purchase. You can use a price comparison app to help with this task.

![Escolhendo os produtos certos](/images/posts/economizar-nas-compras-do-supermercado-2.webp)



Here are some examples of products you can choose to save [money](/en/posts/financial-planning-for-couples):
* Instead of buying whole milk, choose skim milk or powdered milk.
* Instead of buying white bread, choose whole wheat bread or rye bread.
* Instead of buying beef, choose chicken or fish.

## Avoiding Unnecessary Expenses
Another way to save [money](/en/posts/organizing-finances-as-a-freelancer) on supermarket shopping is to avoid unnecessary expenses. This includes not buying products that are not necessary, such as processed foods or alcoholic beverages. Additionally, it's essential to avoid buying products that are on sale but not needed.

For example, if you see a product on sale, but you don't need it, don't buy it. Instead, buy only the products you need and that are within your [budget](/en/posts/50-30-20-rule-complete-guide).

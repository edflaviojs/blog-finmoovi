## Reducing Consumption
Imagine you pay around R$ 200 per month on your electricity bill. This may seem like a reasonable amount, but if you know how to reduce this consumption, you can save around R$ 50 per month, which is equivalent to R$ 600 per year. This can be achieved with a few simple changes in your daily routine. For example, if you have a salary of R$ 5,000 per month, a reduction of R$ 50 on the electricity bill can mean a savings of 1% of your salary.

One of the main reasons for high energy consumption is the excessive use of electronic devices. Many people leave the TV, computer, and other devices on even when they're not in use. This can significantly increase the electricity bill. Additionally, the use of incandescent bulbs can also contribute to high energy consumption.

## Switching Bulbs
Switching incandescent bulbs to LED bulbs can be a great way to reduce energy consumption. LED bulbs consume about 70% less energy than incandescent bulbs and have a longer lifespan. For example, if you have 10 incandescent bulbs in your home and replace them with LED bulbs, you can save around R$ 20 per month.

**Practical Tip:** Check if the bulbs you're buying are actually LED and have energy efficiency certification. This can ensure that you're buying high-quality bulbs that will last longer and save more energy.

## Turning Off Devices
Turning off electronic devices when not in use is another way to reduce energy consumption. Many people leave devices on standby, which still consumes energy. Turning off devices completely can save around R$ 10 per month.

Additionally, it's essential to check if the devices you're buying have energy efficiency certification. This can ensure that you're buying devices that will consume less energy and last longer.

## Daily Savings
Here are some tips to save energy in your daily routine:
* Turn off lights and devices when not in use
* Use LED bulbs instead of incandescent bulbs
* Turn off the computer and TV when not in use
* Use a thermostat to control the temperature in your home

## Consumption Control
It's essential to regularly check your electricity bill to know how much you're consuming and where the highest consumption is. This can help identify areas where you can reduce consumption and save money.

![Economia no Dia a Dia](/images/posts/economize-na-conta-de-energia-3.svg)



**Practical Tip:** Check if the electricity company offers a consumption monitoring service. This can help identify areas where you can reduce consumption and save money.

## Start Today
Now that you know how to save on your electricity bill, it's time to start taking action. Start by turning off lights and devices when not in use and replacing incandescent bulbs with LED bulbs. With these simple changes, you can save around R$ 50 per month and achieve your financial goals.

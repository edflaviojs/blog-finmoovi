## Introduction
The July [vacation](/en/posts/en-5-dicas-para-reorganizar-suas-financas-nas-ferias) is a highly anticipated time for many Brazilians, an opportunity to relax, [travel](/en/posts/en-voce-perde-o-controle-dos-gastos-quando-viaja-usando-moedas-), and enjoy time with family and friends. However, it's common for these periods of leisure to be accompanied by excessive spending, compromising the family [budget](/en/posts/en-orcamento-com-salario-minimo-dicas-praticas) and generating financial stress. In this article, we'll explore practical tips to save [money](/en/posts/en-30-day-rule-for-purchases) on your July [vacation](/en/posts/en-5-dicas-para-reorganizar-suas-financas-nas-ferias) without sacrificing fun.

## [Financial Planning](/en/posts/en-5-dicas-para-se-preparar-financeiramente-para-o-segundo-seme)
First and foremost, it's essential to have a solid financial plan. This includes defining a [budget](/en/posts/en-how-to-create-personal-budget) for the vacation, considering all possible expenses, from transportation and accommodation to food and activities. A practical example is the case of a family of four, with a monthly salary of R$ 8,000.00. If they decide to travel to the Northeast, the cost of airfare can range from R$ 500.00 to R$ 1,000.00 per person, depending on the time and airline. Additionally, the cost of accommodation can range from R$ 100.00 to R$ 300.00 per night, depending on the type of accommodation chosen.

## Tips to Save [Money](/en/posts/en-avoid-impulse-purchases)
Here are some practical tips to save [money](/en/posts/en-creating-an-emergency-fund) on your July vacation:
- Plan ahead: The earlier you plan, the more chances you'll have to find deals and discounts.
- Choose more affordable destinations: Instead of choosing international destinations, consider national options that are more affordable.
- Use travel apps: The [FinMoovi](/en/posts/en-5-alternativas-ao-mobills-em-2026) app, for example, can help you find deals and discounts on transportation and accommodation.
- Cook instead of eating out: If you're in an apartment or house, cook instead of eating out to save [money](/en/posts/en-difference-between-investing-and-saving).
- Avoid unnecessary expenses: Be aware of your spending and avoid buying unnecessary things.

## [Budget](/en/posts/en-how-to-create-personal-budget) and [Expense Control](/en/posts/en-voce-tambem-esquece-de-anotar-seus-gastos)
A well-defined [budget](/en/posts/en-how-to-create-personal-budget) and [expense control](/en/posts/en-metodo-50-30-20-para-orcamento) are crucial to saving [money](/en/posts/en-financial-education-for-children) on vacation. The [Banco Central do Brasil](https://www.bcb.gov.br) offers tips and tools to help manage your [budget](/en/posts/en-how-to-create-personal-budget) and control expenses.

## Understanding the [50-30-20 Method](/en/posts/metodo-50-30-20-para-orcamento)
The 50-30-20 method is a simple and effective approach to managing your finances. The idea is to divide your salary into three categories: essential needs (50%), desires and leisure (30%), and [savings](/en/posts/financial-education-for-children) and [investments](/en/posts/previdencia-privada-vs-investir-por-conta-propria-qual-vale-) (20%). ## Allocating Essential Needs
Essential needs include [expenses](/en/posts/5-dicas-para-se-preparar-financeiramente-para-o-segundo-seme) such as rent, water, electricity, internet, food, and transportation. It's essential to identify what your essential needs are and allocate 50% of your salary to these expenses. For example, if you earn R$ 4,000.00 per month, you should allocate R$ 2,000.00 for essential needs.

**Practical tip:** Make a list of all your essential expenses and check if you're within the 50% limit of your salary.

![Alocando Necessidades Essenciais](/images/posts/metodo-50-30-20-para-salario-brasileiro-2.webp)



## Managing Desires and Leisure
The desires and leisure category includes expenses such as travel, clothing purchases, restaurants, and entertainment. It's essential to remember that these expenses should be limited to 30% of your salary. For example, if you earn R$ 4,000.00 per month, you should allocate R$ 1,200.00 for desires and leisure.

| Desires and Leisure | Value (R$) |
| --- | --- |
| Travel | 300.00 |
| Clothing Purchases | 200.00 |
| Restaurants | 300.00 |
| Entertainment | 400.00 |
| Total | 1,200.00 |

## Savings and [Investments](/en/posts/cdb-vs-tesouro-[selic](/en/glossario/en-selic)-qual-e-melhor-para-voce)
The savings and [investments](/en/posts/cdb-vs-tesouro-selic-qual-e-melhor-para-voce) category is crucial for your financial future. It's essential to allocate 20% of your salary to savings and [investments](/en/posts/cdb-vs-tesouro-selic-qual-rende-mais-em-2026). For example, if you earn R$ 4,000.00 per month, you should allocate R$ 800.00 for savings and investments.

**Practical tip:** Open a [savings account](/en/glossario/en-poupanca) and set up an automatic transfer to save 20% of your salary every month.

![Poupança e Investimentos](/images/posts/metodo-50-30-20-para-salario-brasileiro-4.webp)



## Adjusting the 50-30-20 Method
The 50-30-20 method is a flexible approach and can be adjusted according to individual needs. For example, if you have a high-value debt, you can allocate more than 20% of your salary to debt repayment. Or, if you're [saving](/en/posts/criar-reserva-de-emergencia) for a specific goal, such as buying a house, you can allocate more than 20% of your salary to savings.

## Start with 5 minutes a day
Start applying the 50-30-20 method today. Reserve 5 minutes a day to review your finances and adjust your [budget](/en/posts/50-30-20-rule-complete-guide). Over time, you'll become more aware of your expenses and more efficient in managing your finances.

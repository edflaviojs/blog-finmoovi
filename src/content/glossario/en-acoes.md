## What are Stocks?

Stocks are pieces of a company traded on the Stock Exchange (B3 in Brazil). When you buy a stock from Petrobras, for example, you become a minority shareholder of the company.

## How to make money with Stocks

1. **Appreciation:** buy cheap and sell for more
2. **Dividends:** receive part of the company's profits
3. **Juros sobre Capital Próprio (JCP):** similar to dividends, with different tax treatment

## Risks

- The price can drop (you can lose money)
- Companies can go bankrupt
- High volatility in the short term

## Who is it for?

Stocks are suitable for those who:
- Already have an emergency fund
- Have a long-term horizon (5+ years)
- Accept short-term fluctuations
- Want returns above fixed income (e.g., CDI, CDB, LCI, LCA) and can handle the risks, such as the IOF tax, and can use payment methods like PIX to invest

## How to start

1. Open an account with a brokerage firm (XP, Clear, Rico, etc.)
2. Study companies before investing
3. Start with a small amount (approximately $25 USD, as $100 BRL is roughly $25 USD, is possible)
4. Diversify across different sectors, considering the IPCA inflation index and ETF options

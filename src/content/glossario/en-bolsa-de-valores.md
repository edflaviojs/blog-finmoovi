## What is the Stock Exchange?

The Stock Exchange is like a market where companies sell pieces of themselves (stocks) and investors buy and sell these pieces among themselves. In Brazil, the stock exchange is called **B3** (Brasil, Bolsa, Balcão).

## How it works

1. Companies go public (IPO) to raise money
2. Investors buy stocks of these companies
3. The stocks are traded daily on the exchange
4. The price goes up and down according to supply and demand

## Operating hours

The B3 operates from Monday to Friday:
- **Pre-market:** 9:45 to 10:00
- **Regular trading:** 10:00 to 17:00
- **After-hours market:** 17:30 to 18:00

## Main indexes

- **Ibovespa:** index with the most traded stocks in Brazil
- **IFIX:** real estate investment fund index
- **S&P 500:** main US index

## How to invest in the stock market

You need an account with a brokerage firm. Most firms do not charge a fee to open an account. With approximately $25 USD (R$ 100) you can already buy fractional stocks.
